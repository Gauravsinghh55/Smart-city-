Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our citizen complaint and feedback platform.

To submit a complaint, navigate to the "Report Issue" page using the main navigation menu. Select the appropriate category for your complaint (e.g., potholes, sanitation, water supply), complete all required fields including a description and location, upload any supporting images or videos, and click the "Submit" button. You'll receive a reference number for tracking the status of your complaint.

Go to the "Track Status" page and enter your complaint reference number. The system will display the current status of your complaint (open, in progress, or resolved), an estimated resolution time, and any comments from authorities handling your case. You can also opt-in for email or SMS notifications to receive real-time updates about status changes.

Yes, we take data security very seriously. Our platform uses SSL encryption to protect all data transmissions, and we follow strict data protection protocols. Your personal information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel. For more details, please visit our "Security" page.

While we encourage users to provide contact information for follow-up purposes, you can submit complaints with minimal personal information. However, completely anonymous submissions may limit our ability to request additional details or provide you with status updates. For sensitive issues, we recommend using our confidential reporting option, which protects your identity while still allowing for necessary communication.

Our platform supports reporting of various civic issues including but not limited to:

  • Road maintenance (potholes, damaged sidewalks)
  • Sanitation issues (garbage collection, public cleanliness)
  • Water supply problems
  • Street lighting failures
  • Public transportation concerns
  • Environmental issues (pollution, illegal dumping)
  • Public safety concerns

If your issue doesn't fit into these categories, you can select "Other" and provide details.

Resolution times vary depending on the nature and complexity of the issue. When you track your complaint, the system provides an estimated resolution time based on similar past cases. Urgent matters related to public safety are prioritized for immediate attention. You can always check the current status of your complaint using your reference number on the Track Status page.

We welcome your feedback! Navigate to the "Feedback" section in the main menu, where you can rate your experience, provide comments, and suggest improvements. You can choose to submit feedback anonymously or include your contact information if you'd like us to follow up with you. Your input helps us continually enhance the platform.

Several resources are available to help you:

  • Our chatbot assistant is available on every page to answer common questions
  • Video tutorials are provided for each major feature
  • For additional assistance, you can contact our support team via the "Contact Us" form or call our helpline at (123) 456-7890 during business hours
